What is CodeFii?

CodeFii is an app that allows users to upload the problemsets on the cloud which the students can solve.

How do I add Problemsets/Assignments?

First, create a new problemset by filling out the form on this page. Once an problemset is created you will be able to add/upload problems.

What are some Problemset logo best practices?

  • Have a resolution of 512x512
  • Use common image formats such as .JPG, .GIF, or .PNG
  • Remain under the 2MB limit.
  • Square images look best
